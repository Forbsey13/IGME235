Name

Cursor In Danger

Concept

A Sidescrolling game where there are various abstacles that will be appearing in the screen. These items will general be coming from the right to left and you have to move the cursor to avoid them. Similar to flappy bird but instead of physics mechanics a harder precision based game using the mouse

Platform

Desktop Web (Need Mouse)

Story

You are trying to escape the onslaught of garbage that an alien is throwing out after his visit to Earth (Subject to change)

Aesthetic

Simple graphics depicting pipes/garbage bags/etc. that the mouse will have to dodge

Gameplay

The game will be described with instructions and moving the mouse is something that most people know how to do. The player could chose their difficulty

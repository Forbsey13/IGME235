Rocket League

Publishing Info

  • Published by: Psyonix, Inc.
  • Developed by: Psyonix, Inc.
  • Released: July, 2015

Description

It is a vehicle based, futuristic variant of soccer. Just like the original game it is played in a large arena with two teams of cars. As with soccer the goal is to score by launching a giant ball into the goal of the opposing team. The arena has curved edges with large walls, so the ball cannot leave the pitch and bounces back, and it is possible to drive up the sides.

Game Categories

  • Genre: Racing/Driving, Sports
  • Perspective: Behind View
  • Sport: Soccer
  • Online Players: 2-8
